# Vessel TSS Rules & Behaviour

| | Container | Fishing | Ferry | Recreational (Yacht, Small Motorboats) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Enter TSS** | Everywhere | Can use for navigating but must not conduct fishing activities | Cross inland strait | Outside TSS / Cross inland strait if crossing other side |
| **Direction** | TSS | TSS if navigating within. Fishing just outside the TSS. You can simulate some violating by entering the TSS from edges | Across TSS | N/A / Across TSS (if crossing other side) |
| **Speed** | Slow | Fast / Slow | Medium | Slow / Medium |
| **Heading** | Slow | Fast | Fast | Fast |
| **Momentum** | High | Low | Medium | High |
| **Distance Keeping** | 800 m inside TSS. Lateral distance can be as narrow as 50–60 m while overtaking | Same rules inside TSS. If engaged in fishing, keep at least 1000 m away from the aft | They arrange their speed, heading to pass fore and aft of TSS vessels. Distance crossing at aft can be as short as 50 m. Distance crossing in front of OS can be around 200–300 m. | They arrange their speed, heading to pass fore and aft of TSS vessels. Distance crossing at aft can be as short as 50 m. Distance crossing in front of OS can be around 200–300 m. |
| **Path Following** | Yes | Yes (navigating) / No (engaged in fishing) | Yes | No |
| **Obey TSS Direction** | Yes | Yes (navigating) / No (engaged in fishing) | No | N/A |
